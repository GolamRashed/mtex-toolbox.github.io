max

(method of PoleFigure)

maximum of two pole figures or the maximum of a single polefigure

Syntax 

m  = max(pf)
pf = max(pf1,pf2)
pf = max(pf1,x) = max(x,pf1)

Input

pf

PoleFigure
pf1, pf2

PoleFigure
x

double

Output

m

double
pf

PoleFigure
