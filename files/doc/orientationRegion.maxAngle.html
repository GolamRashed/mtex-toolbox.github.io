maxAngle

(method of orientationRegion)

get the maximum angle of the fundamental region

Syntax 

omega = maxAngle(oR)   % maximum angle in the orientation region
omega = maxAngle(oR,v) % maximum angle about axis v

Input

oR

orientationRegion
v

vector3d
